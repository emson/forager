The new Bugaboo Cameleon now comes with Improved handle adjustments and foam filled tyres so no more punctures. Designed to prowl the urban jungle, the large wheels with their efficient suspension eat up pavements, kerbs and potholes with equal ease, while the small, swivelling front wheels help maneouvre beautifully. Stray off road and the Cameleon morphs into the woods mode with the large wheels now leading to offer superb off the road handling. A pushchair for all terrains the Cameleon will even tackle the beach with its unique beach mode facility.

Choose your base colour (Red, Blue, Dark Grey or Sand) and then choose your tailored fleece or fabric fabric available in various different colours to personalise your travel system.

Carrycot

The carrycot on your Cameleon chassis can be used from birth until your baby is approximately 6 months old. Your baby can recline totally flat and thanks to the Aerosleep® mattress, baby remains comfy, cool and protected from dust, pollen and house dust mites. You can also use the carrycot by itself in place of a moses basket for the first couple of months.

Seat Unit

The seat unit can be rear or forward facing from approximately 6 months. Theres an integral 5-point harness and 3 seat positions. The seat can also be used separately from the chassis as a handy seat for feeding or just sitting !
